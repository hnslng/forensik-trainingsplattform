<h2 class="section-title"><span class="number">15.3</span> Filter &ndash; Das Rauschen entfernen</h2><p>Ohne Filter siehst du Tausende Pakete pro Minute. Filter helfen, nur das zu zeigen was relevant ist.</p><p><strong>Zwei Arten von Filtern:</strong></p><div class="table-container"><table><thead><tr><th>Typ</th><th>Wann angewendet</th><th>Syntax</th><th>Beispiel</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><strong>Capture-Filter</strong> (<span class="inline-code">-f</span>)</td><td>Beim Mitschneiden</td><td>BPF-Syntax</td><td><span class="inline-code">port 53</span></td></tr><tr><td><strong>Display-Filter</strong> (<span class="inline-code">-Y</span>)</td><td>Nach dem Capture</td><td>Wireshark-Syntax</td><td><span class="inline-code">dns</span> oder <span class="inline-code">tcp.port == 80</span></td></tr></tbody></table></div><p><strong>Wichtige Display-Filter:</strong></p><div class="table-container"><table><thead><tr><th>Filter</th><th>Zeigt</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><span class="inline-code">tcp.port == 80</span></td><td>Nur HTTP-Traffic</td></tr><tr><td><span class="inline-code">ip.addr == 192.168.1.1</span></td><td>Nur Traffic von/zu dieser IP</td></tr><tr><td><span class="inline-code">dns</span></td><td>Nur DNS-Anfragen und -Antworten</td></tr><tr><td><span class="inline-code">http.request.method == "POST"</span></td><td>Nur POST-Requests (z.B. Logins)</td></tr><tr><td><span class="inline-code">tcp.flags.syn == 1</span></td><td>Nur Verbindungsaufbau (SYN)</td></tr><tr><td><span class="inline-code">arp</span></td><td>Nur ARP-Anfragen</td></tr></tbody></table></div><p><strong>Kombinieren:</strong> <span class="inline-code">ip.addr == 10.0.0.1 && tcp.port == 443</span> = Nur HTTPS-Traffic mit dieser IP</p>
